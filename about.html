Hi, I'm Shuayb

I’m a Computer Science graduate who enjoys writing about life, career struggles, technology, and the quiet thoughts we usually keep to ourselves. This blog is my space to be honest, reflective, and real about my journey.

My Journey

I graduated in Computer Science and Engineering in 2023. Like many freshers, I stepped into the professional world with expectations, uncertainty, and a lot of questions. Over time, I realized that finding the right path isn’t always straightforward. Through different experiences, interviews, rejections, and reflections, I’ve been learning more about what I want — and what I don’t.

This blog is where I document those lessons. Some posts are about work and careers, some about personal experiences, and some about the small moments that shape how we see ourselves and the world.

Why This Blog

I believe that many of us go through similar doubts but rarely talk about them openly. If something I write helps you feel understood, less alone, or more confident in your own path, then this space is doing its job.
